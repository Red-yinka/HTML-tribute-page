Pastor Faith A. OYEDEPO

A woman who inspired and fired the imagination
of millions of Christian women worldwide.

She is a pastor, teacher, and a role model to millions of Christian women worldwide. She has in her own way managed to help her husband develop his ministry. Her contributed efforts in the works of God has made her do what seems impossible for a woman. She is currently the Vice President (Education) of the Living Faith Church Worldwide and the Vice Chancellor of Convenant University.
